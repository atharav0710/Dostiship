Our Services

Discover how Dostiship empowers introverted college students to connect, grow, and thrive through innovative features designed for meaningful friendships.

In-App Journal

Track your social journey with a private journal. Reflect on interactions, set connection goals, and celebrate milestones to boost confidence and personal growth.

Icebreaker Challenges

Start conversations effortlessly with fun, guided prompts like “What’s your favorite study snack?”—perfect for introverts to break the ice without stress.

Personality-Based Matching

Find friends who truly get you through quizzes matching interests (e.g., anime, coding) and personality traits, ensuring authentic and compatible connections.

Virtual Hangout Spaces

Join themed online rooms (e.g., Music Jam, Study Buddy) for low-pressure chats and activities, making bonding fun and comfortable from anywhere.

Interactive Map

Discover nearby students with shared interests or moods on a real-time map, making it easy to plan spontaneous meetups or study sessions.

Senior Mentorship

Get personalized guidance from experienced seniors on academics, career paths, and college life through a unique skill-based mentorship system.

Community Meetups

Attend curated virtual or in-person events, like study mixers or hobby clubs, to build lasting friendships in a safe, inclusive environment.
